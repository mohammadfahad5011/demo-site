ABOUT US...

We are a NYC based construction company. Our values have stayed the same since the first day. We strive to provide immaculate service with haggle free pricing which satisfies our customers. In this 14 years of experience we have specialized in various fields of construction such as: brownstone, bathroom renovations, kitchen renovations, home additions, machinery/concrete work, painting, dry wall, roofing, plumping and also electrical work. Since the company was started with family we treat our employees like family. This relationship helped us build a trustworthy team of workers who handle all projects with professionalism and care. The extent of care and detail we provide our customers is unmatched. Our business has mainly developed through customer satisfaction, on time completion of work and the quality of service we provide. We have completed projects of various sizes. Starting from small home renovations to large government contracts. Our portfolio covers all 5 boroughs of NYC, Long Island, New Jersey and upstate New York.In doing so we treat all projects, big or small, with the same attention to detail and quality of work with intensive care.
